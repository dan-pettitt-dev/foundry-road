Where We Are

The pre-school is situated in rooms behind St. Andrew's Church, Foundry Road, Wall Heath. We have two well equipped and spacious rooms, and a large room for cafe. We have a secure outside play area with spongy floor. We also have the use of the large church hall, especially when outside play is not possible.
